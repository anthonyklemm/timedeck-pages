FAQ

  • YouTube shows errors / empty player
    Some videos may be unavailable in your region. Use Open in YouTube to play the full list in the YouTube app.
  • Apple Music sign-in doesn’t finish
    Make sure you’re signed in to Apple Music on the device. If it still loops, try: Settings → Safari → Clear Website Data, then try again.
  • Playlist added fewer tracks than expected
    Not every song has a perfect Apple Music match in every region. We add everything we can find automatically.
  • Does TapeDeck stream Apple Music?
    No. We create a playlist in your library via Apple’s official MusicKit APIs.
