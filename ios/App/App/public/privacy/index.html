Privacy Policy

Effective:

What we do

TapeDeck Time Machine generates tracklists from historical charts and helps you create matching playlists in your Apple Music library.

Data we collect

  • Apple Music User Token (via MusicKit). Used solely to create the playlist you request. We do not store it long-term.
  • Usage analytics (page views/events) via Google Analytics to improve the product. No cross-app tracking.

Data we do not collect

  • No passwords or Apple IDs
  • No contact lists or precise location
  • No advertising identifiers for tracking

How we use your data

  • Create a playlist in your Apple Music library at your request
  • Diagnose reliability issues and improve features

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We use service providers (e.g., Apple Music APIs, YouTube iframe, Google Analytics) to deliver features.

Security

Transport is encrypted over HTTPS. Tokens are handled securely and only used for the immediate playlist request.

Your choices

  • You can revoke Apple Music access at any time from Apple Music account settings.
  • Contact us to request deletion of any server logs that may contain your IP or request metadata.
