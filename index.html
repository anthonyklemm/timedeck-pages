Why does this exist?

Curated playlists miss the messy magic of real radio. We recreate the flow: currents, recurrents, gold, and the occasional weird deep cut—grounded by the songs that actually charted. The result feels familiar, not generic.

Your privacy matters. We don’t proxy audio or scrape your accounts. Playback happens through official embeds and your own subscriptions.

How it works

1) Simulate

Choose date + genre. We build a day’s worth of spins from the chart week using radio-style rotations.

2) Resolve

We map tracks to playable sources. Our cache finds official videos fast; search fills gaps.

3) Play & Export

Listen in the browser via YouTube. Apple/Spotify tabs let subscribers stream and export the playlist.
