Privacy Policy & Terms

Effective:

What we do

TapeDeck Time Machine generates tracklists from historical charts and helps you create matching playlists in your **Apple Music** or **Spotify** library.

Data we collect

  • Apple Music User Token (via MusicKit). Used solely to create the playlist you request. We do not store it long-term.
  • Spotify Access Token (via Spotify OAuth). We use this to get your Spotify User ID and create playlists. This token is stored in your browser's localStorage to keep you signed in, but is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage analytics (page views/events) via Google Analytics to improve the product. No cross-app tracking.

Data we do not collect

  • No passwords or Apple IDs
  • No contact lists or precise location
  • No advertising identifiers for tracking

How we use your data

  • Create a playlist in your Apple Music or Spotify library at your request.
  • Diagnose reliability issues and improve features.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We use service providers (e.g., Apple Music APIs, **Spotify APIs**, YouTube iframe, Google Analytics) to deliver features.

Your choices

  • You can revoke Apple Music access at any time from Apple Music account settings.
  • You can revoke Spotify access at any time from your Spotify Account Apps page.
  • Contact us to request deletion of any server logs that may contain your IP or request metadata.
